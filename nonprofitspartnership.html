Non-Profits @ The E

The Emerald Foundation “E”
Partnership Program:

We are also hosting weekly and as needed partnered non-profit programming at greatly reduced or free space. Current programming includes:


Learning support for children and adults with intellectual disabilities

Utilizing the building in collaboration with local schools to teach life skills such as cleaning, basic cooking, set up and tear down of events, mailings etc.


After-school education and feeding program 2017/2018 school year

After a very successful pilot program in 2017, the after school program will be expanded to include up to 60 2nd-6th grade students for 2017-2018 school year. These students will be hand selected by the principals and Manheim Township School District and The Mix at Arbor Place to provide academic enrichment, as well as social and recreational activities to children that would not otherwise have this access. Enrichment programming include Drug Prevention programming from Caron for both the children and their parents, tennis lessons from Tennis Central, and several music and arts programs. Food will be provided by the Pennsylvania Food Bank.


T. Alexander Foundation – Preparing Athletes for life after sport

Camp, workshop and after school programs helping middle and high school emerging scholar athletes with an array of academic, athletic and life skills to achieve a lifetime of success on and off their respective playing – For more information go click here, or email Tracy Jones at tjones@talexanderfoundation.org


Youth Advocate Programs (YAP)

Young Adult Programming – for more information please go to: http://yapinc.org


Technology training:

Collaboration with a start-up company (CrowdJoy) that is developing augmented reality apps, with high school students utilizing our tech center to do geo-mapping


In addition, we offer non-profits reduced rental fees for ballroom, conference room and gym on as-needed basis.

If you are a non-profit and are interested in holding programming at “The E”, please contact Laurie Cubell at Lcubell@emeraldfoundationpa.org
If you are a non-profit and are interested in renting space for an event, please contact our event director, Leraunt at events@emeraldfoundationpa.org
